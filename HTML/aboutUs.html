About Us

Our mission

At Gamescom, we're dedicated to celebrating the vibrant culture and innovation of the gaming industry.
Our mission is to provide a platform where players, creators, and industry professionals can come together to explore the latest advancements,
connect with like-minded individuals, and immerse themselves in the world of gaming.

What we offer

Gamescom 2024 promises an unparalleled experience for attendees of all backgrounds and interests.
Whether you're a casual gamer, a competitive esports enthusiast, a seasoned developer, or a curious newcomer, there's something here for you.

  • Expo Hall: Explore our expansive expo hall featuring the latest games, cutting-edge technology, and innovative products from top industry players.

  • Developer Conference: Dive deep into the world of game development with insightful panels, workshops, and presentations led by industry experts.

  • Esports Arena: Witness thrilling esports competitions live on stage or participate in tournaments to showcase your skills and compete for glory.

  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow gamers, developers, publishers, and influencers to share ideas, forge partnerships, and expand
    your professional network.

  • Exclusive Reveals: Be the first to discover upcoming releases, exclusive content, and exciting announcements from leading game studios/publishers.
